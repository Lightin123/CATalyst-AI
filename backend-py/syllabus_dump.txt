Objectives:
The objectives of this course are: 
To enable students to use fundamental tools of mathematics in theoretical studies as well as to apply modern computer software to enhance the project-based learning.
To improve the analytical skills needed to solve engineering problems
To provide knowledge and understanding the concepts partial derivatives, multiple integrals and vector Calculus. 
To understand the concepts of Fourier series and application to mechanical systems and electric circuits 
Develop the ability to comprehend and generate rigorous formal mathematical arguments, as well as implement basic mathematical logic.
The usage of MATLAB to facilitate understanding and     visualization of mathematical problems.
